# SBTI Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 5, 2026

SBTI is a lighthearted personality quiz app. We designed it to work without accounts, advertising, analytics SDKs, or cross-app tracking.

## Information We Do Not Collect

SBTI does not collect personally identifiable information, contact information, precise location, photos, contacts, payment information, advertising identifiers, or user-generated content.

The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

## Information Stored On Your Device

SBTI stores a small amount of app-only data on your device, such as your selected language and cached ranking data. This information is stored with iOS user defaults and is not shared with other apps.

## Photo Library Permission

If you choose to save your result card, SBTI asks for add-only access to your photo library. This permission is used only to save the generated result image. SBTI does not read, upload, analyze, or share your existing photos.

## Network Access

SBTI may request public ranking data from:

`https://api.fmoconsole.cn/api/ranking`

This request is used to display the in-app popularity ranking. If the request fails, the app uses local fallback data. The app does not send quiz answers, result cards, account information, or personal profile data with this request. As with normal internet traffic, the service provider may receive basic technical request information such as IP address and user agent for security and service operation. This information is not used by SBTI for advertising or tracking.

## Children

SBTI is intended for general entertainment. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes

We may update this policy if SBTI's privacy practices change. The updated policy will be published at the same privacy policy URL.

## Contact

For privacy or support questions, please contact us through:

https://github.com/17classdeveloper-design/SBTI/issues

# SBTI 隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 6 月 5 日

SBTI 是一款轻松娱乐向的人格测试应用。我们设计它时不需要账号、不接入广告、不接入分析 SDK，也不进行跨 App 追踪。

## 我们不收集的信息

SBTI 不收集可识别个人身份的信息、联系方式、精确位置、照片、通讯录、支付信息、广告标识符或用户生成内容。

本 App 不使用第三方分析、广告或追踪 SDK。

## 存储在你设备上的信息

SBTI 会在你的设备本地存储少量仅供 App 自身使用的数据，例如语言选择和排行榜缓存。这些信息通过 iOS 用户默认设置保存，不会与其他 App 共享。

## 相册权限

当你选择保存测试结果卡片时，SBTI 会请求“仅添加”到相册的权限。该权限只用于保存生成的结果图片。SBTI 不会读取、上传、分析或分享你已有的照片。

## 网络访问

SBTI 可能会向以下地址请求公开排行榜数据：

`https://api.fmoconsole.cn/api/ranking`

该请求只用于展示 App 内的热门排行。如果请求失败，App 会使用本地备用数据。App 不会通过该请求发送你的答题内容、结果卡、账号信息或个人资料。和一般网络访问一样，服务提供方可能因安全和服务运行需要接收 IP 地址、User-Agent 等基础技术请求信息；SBTI 不会将这些信息用于广告或追踪。

## 儿童隐私

SBTI 面向一般娱乐用途。我们不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。

## 政策变更

如果 SBTI 的隐私实践发生变化，我们会更新本政策，并在同一隐私政策地址发布新版内容。

## 联系方式

如有隐私或支持问题，请通过以下地址联系我们：

https://github.com/17classdeveloper-design/SBTI/issues
