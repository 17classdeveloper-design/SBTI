SBTI Support

If you need help with SBTI, please open an issue:

https://github.com/17classdeveloper-design/SBTI/issues

Common Topics

  • Saving a result card to Photos
  • Changing the app language
  • Ranking data not refreshing
  • App Store review or privacy questions
  • Reporting a typo or localization issue

Please include your device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of the issue.
